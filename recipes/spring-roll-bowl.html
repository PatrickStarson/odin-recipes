Spring Roll Bowl

Description

A Spring Roll Bowl is a fresh, deconstructed take on traditional spring rolls. It combines noodles or rice, crisp veggies, protein, and a flavorful dressing for a quick, healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles or jasmine rice
  • Cooked shrimp, tofu, or grilled chicken
  • Shredded carrots
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • Bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • Fresh mint and cilantro
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Lime wedges
  • Optional: peanut sauce or hoisin-based dressing

Steps

  1. Cook rice noodles or jasmine rice according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare your protein of choice.
  3. Slice all vegetables into thin strips.
  4. Add noodles or rice to a bowl as the base.
  5. Layer on veggies and protein.
  6. Top with herbs, peanuts, and lime wedges.
  7. Drizzle with sauce before serving.

Salmon Quinoa Power Bowl

Description

A nourishing and colorful bowl loaded with protein, healthy fats, and fiber. This dish features a base of fluffy quinoa topped with fresh salmon, avocado, edamame, radishes, and a dollop of hummus — perfect for a light but satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Cooked quinoa (white, red, or tri-color)
  • Raw or lightly cured salmon, diced
  • Avocado, cubed
  • Edamame (cooked and shelled)
  • Radishes, thinly sliced
  • Hummus
  • Black sesame seeds (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool slightly.
  2. Slice radishes thinly, cube the avocado, and prepare the edamame.
  3. Arrange quinoa in a bowl as the base.
  4. Add salmon, avocado, edamame, and radish slices on top.
  5. Spoon hummus on the side.
  6. Sprinkle with black sesame seeds and serve.

Crispy Tofu Glow Bowl

A vibrant, plant-based bowl full of flavor and texture. This dish combines crispy tofu with fresh veggies, seeds, and a creamy green dressing — perfect for a wholesome, energizing meal.

Description

Ingredients

  • Crispy tofu cubes
  • Arugula or mixed greens
  • Shredded carrots
  • Sliced avocado
  • Pickled red cabbage
  • Shredded radish
  • Sprouts (e.g. alfalfa or broccoli)
  • Sunflower seeds
  • Creamy green dressing (e.g. tahini-herb or avocado-lime)

Steps

  1. Press and cube tofu, then bake or pan-fry until golden and crispy.
  2. Wash and prep all vegetables.
  3. Place arugula or greens in a bowl as the base.
  4. Arrange tofu, avocado, carrots, cabbage, radish, and sprouts on top.
  5. Sprinkle with sunflower seeds.
  6. Drizzle or serve with your favorite creamy green dressing.
